# Document Summarizer + Action Extractor

**An AI-powered tool that reads your business documents in seconds and tells you exactly what happened, what needs to be done, and what's still unresolved.**

Built for business owners and operations teams at small and mid-sized companies who don't have time to read everything that lands on their desk.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Right now, reading a 20-page board memo takes 45 minutes. This tool does it in 8 seconds.

Most business documents — board memos, supplier contracts, project reports, meeting notes — contain a handful of things that actually matter: a few decisions, a few action items, a few open questions. The rest is context and filler. This tool finds the signal and discards the noise, automatically.

---

## What You Can Use It For

**Board memos and meeting minutes**
Drop in the memo from your last board meeting and get a clean breakdown of every decision made, every action assigned, and every question left unanswered — without reading through pages of background.

**Supplier and vendor contracts**
Quickly surface what's being agreed to, who's responsible for what, and what terms remain unresolved before a contract goes to legal.

**Project status reports**
Paste in a weekly project update and immediately see what's on track, what's at risk, and what your team is waiting on.

**Internal briefings and strategy documents**
Before a leadership meeting, run your briefing document through the tool and walk in already knowing the key points — in under 30 seconds.

---

## What It Outputs

Every document is analyzed and returned as four structured panels:

**Summary**
A plain-English overview of the document in 3 to 5 sentences. What is this document about, and why does it matter?

**Action Items**
A table of every task mentioned in the document, along with who is responsible and when it's due. No more digging through paragraphs to find the "next steps."

**Key Decisions**
A bulleted list of every conclusion, approval, or commitment made in the document. Useful for anyone who wasn't in the room.

**Open Questions**
Everything flagged as unresolved, pending, or still to be determined. This is often the most valuable section — it shows you where the gaps are before they become problems.
