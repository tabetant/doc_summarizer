Project Kickoff Meeting Notes
Acme Corp — Atlas Platform Migration
Date: March 17, 2026
Attendees: Sarah Chen (PM), Marcus Rivera (Engineering Lead), Priya Patel (DevOps), Tom Walsh (Finance), Linda Okafor (QA)

---

MEETING SUMMARY

The team convened to kick off the migration of Acme's legacy Atlas platform to the new cloud-based infrastructure. The migration has been approved by the executive team and is expected to reduce infrastructure costs by 40% annually. The project will run in two phases: data migration (Q2) and application cutover (Q3). All departments affected by downtime must be notified at least two weeks in advance.

---

DECISIONS MADE

1. The team decided to use AWS over Azure as the cloud provider, based on existing vendor relationships and the DevOps team's familiarity with AWS tooling.
2. A phased migration approach was approved — no big-bang cutover. Phase 1 covers read-only services; Phase 2 covers transactional systems.
3. The QA team will own end-to-end regression testing before each phase cutover. No phase goes live without QA sign-off.
4. All legacy Atlas data will be retained in cold storage for a minimum of 18 months post-migration for compliance purposes.
